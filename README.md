# Amoxtli Partners

Amoxtli Partners is a private referral management workspace used internally to coordinate how the company collaborates with external partners. The app centralizes partner submissions, review workflows, and payouts so the team can track each opportunity end-to-end without exposing sensitive customer or financial data.

## What It Does

- Provides a dedicated workspace where partners can submit referrals and monitor progress through a transparent, read-only view.
- Gives Amoxtli staff a secure dashboard to review submissions, approve or reject leads, and record important context.
- Tracks commissions and payouts in one place so both sides know when work has been accepted and when payments are scheduled or completed.

## Audience & Access

This project is intended for Amoxtli's internal team and trusted partners only. Access is invite-based, and operational or infrastructure details are intentionally omitted from this repository. Reach out to the Amoxtli team through existing internal channels if you need onboarding guidance or support.
